Valeriana Wellness Lounge

Experience relaxation and regeneration for the body and soul in our Vajolet Wellness Lounge.
We bring nature into our wellness lounge with select and natural materials from the region. Our holiday apartments with wellness in South Tyrol ensure guests a wonderful state of relaxation. Here we deliberately rely on the elements of our mountain landscape: pure mountain water, fresh Alpine air, powerful earth, and the warmth of fire characterise the spa. Between 3 p.m. and 9 p.m., you can enjoy our pampering offer.

With a Finnish sauna, steam bath and a relaxation rooms, you can really recharge your batteries for the next mountain adventure. Our massages and treatments are uniquely effective and relaxing thanks to the natural products we use. Relaxing moments await you!
